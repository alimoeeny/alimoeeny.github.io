I'm currently working on [HappyPathology](https://happypathology.com) — a tool built to fix a workflow that has barely changed in 30 years.

---

Most clinical labs still process fax and paper orders like it's 1996.

Someone reads the fax.  
Someone types the data into the system.  
Someone double-checks it.

This happens for **every** order.

This workflow doesn't scale. Growth meant hiring more people to type.

![There's a better way](/images/theresabtterway.jpg)

Until now.

HappyPathology converts paper, fax, and PDFs into organized clinical data, and routes it to the systems your lab already uses.

No re-entry. Already searchable.
